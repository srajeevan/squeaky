How do I unlock everything?

The first pack (Closet) is free, plus a sample of the Kitchen. Tap Unlock Everything on the shop or any locked item to buy the one-time "Everything, forever" purchase — every pack, forever, with no ads or subscription.

I paid but my packs are locked

Open Settings → Restore Purchase. Purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you bought it on.

What are bubbles and capsules?

Bubbles are an in-game currency you earn just by playing — they're never bought with real money. Spend them in the shop on cosmetics, or collect free reward capsules every few cleans. Cosmetics are looks only; they don't affect gameplay.

Does Squeaky collect my data?

No. Squeaky collects nothing — no accounts, no analytics, no ads. See our Privacy Policy.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone running iOS 17 or later. It's best enjoyed with headphones for the full ASMR effect.

How do I reset my progress?

Deleting and reinstalling the app clears all local progress. (There's no cloud save, so this can't be undone.)
