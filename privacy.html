Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Squeaky collects no personal data. No accounts, no login, no ads, and no cross-app tracking. The game itself runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

No personal data — no name, email, contacts, or location, ever. The one exception is in-app purchases (see below): to process and measure them we use RevenueCat, which receives your purchase transaction and an anonymous, randomly-generated identifier tied to your device — never to you personally. We never see who you are or how you play the game.

What stays on your device

Your progress, bubbles, cosmetics and settings are saved locally on your iPhone so the game remembers where you left off. This information never leaves your device and is not sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store — Squeaky never sees or stores your payment information. We use RevenueCat to validate purchases, restore them across your devices, and measure aggregate sales. RevenueCat receives the purchase transaction and an anonymous identifier tied to your device, not to you personally. This is used only for purchase functionality and sales analytics — never for advertising and never shared for cross-app tracking.

Children

Squeaky is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data — only anonymous purchase data via RevenueCat — we collect no personal data from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page. Since the app collects no personal data, we don't expect meaningful changes.

Contact

Questions? Reach us any time at propcapitol@gmail.com or on the support page.
