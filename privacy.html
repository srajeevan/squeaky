Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Squeaky collects no personal data. None. The game runs entirely on your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Squeaky has no accounts, no login, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs. We never see who you are or how you play.

What stays on your device

Your progress, bubbles, cosmetics and settings are saved locally on your iPhone so the game remembers where you left off. This information never leaves your device and is not sent to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Purchases

In-app purchases are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Squeaky never sees or stores your payment information. Apple's handling of purchases is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

Children

Squeaky is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. Because we collect no data whatsoever, we collect none from children either.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we'll update this page. Since the app collects no data, we don't expect meaningful changes.

Contact

Questions? Reach us any time at propcapitol@gmail.com or on the support page.
